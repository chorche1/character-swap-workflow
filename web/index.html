B-roll generator

Drop a narration audio file (or video — we'll extract the audio). Whisper transcribes it, GPT-4o plans cinematic medical-realism B-roll prompts, each becomes a clip via your chosen video model, and we stitch + mux your narration over the result.

What are the 4 modes? (visual styles GPT-4o picks per line)
Mode 1
Body Part Transformation — a single close-up where unhealthy tissue visibly changes to healthy across the clip's time. For lines about specific organs / symptoms.
Mode 2
Biological Process — close visualization of an internal process (blood flowing, fat shrinking, inflammation receding). For lines that describe a mechanism.
Mode 3
Anatomical Flythrough — drone-style camera moving through a real anatomical space (blood vessel, spine, alveoli). For lines about journey / scale.
Mode 4
Contextual Human Moment — tight close-up on one body part of a person experiencing the problem or result. For emotional / lifestyle lines.
